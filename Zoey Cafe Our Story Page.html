Our Legacy of 20 Years

Zoey Café is a service-oriented organization that has been serving the people of Iligan City for almost 20 years. Zoey Café was created thru the idea of the booming coffee industry in the country. With this in mind, we wanted to seize the opportunity and so we made our aspiration into a reality. Thus, we celebrated the birth of Zoey Café on July 8, 2002.

In early 2013, we changed owner ship from sole proprietor to partner-ship and was named, Zoey Café, Co.

And so in June 8, 2013 we opened our first branch in Ubaldo Laya, Iligan City.

This branch has a spacious function hall that offers catering services for all occasions.

Since the idea of a coffee shop in the city was new and not many people then appreciated coffee concoctions, we created an extensive menu that not only catered sandwiches and pasta but also included full course meals. Since then we have captured the hearts and tastes of Iliganons by introducing our continental and international menus that are not customary here in Iligan.


Our goal is to extend our catering services outside our restaurants and provide a more flexible catering service to our clients. Zoey Café, Co. is fully committed in providing personalized service to its customers; absolutely taking into consideration the quality and value for money at all times. With this endeavor we want to be recognized and respected as one of the premiere food servers in the city and hopefully in the country.
