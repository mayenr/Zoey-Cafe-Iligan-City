Terms and Conditions

  • 50% Down Payment from contract price must be made upon signing and confirmation of contract or to secure booking
  • 50% Full Payment on the Day of the Event
  • 20% of the contract price will be forfeited for any cancellation made within seven (7) days prior to event date.
  • 30% surcharge for cancellation made within five (5) days
  • 50% charge for cancellation made within 3 days from event date
  • 100% from contract price will be charged for cancellation made on the day of the event
  • Zoey Cafe, Co. is not liable for any food provided by the client that may cause hazard for human consumption. A waiver form must be signed by the client for any food brought to the event.
  • Any losses, breakages of the caterer's equipment during the event will be charged to our client accordingly
  • Surcharge of 2000php for rebooking/change of event three (3) days prior to the original event date
  • Event will include four (4) hours use of the venue. Any succeeding hour will be charged 2000php accordingly; for events past 10:00pm, the charge shall be 3000php per hour
